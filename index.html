About me

My name is Tarmo Lepik. I am a highly qualified jeweler, mostly I make 3 D models of jewelry. Knowledge of 3d modeling programs - matrix, magics, ZBrush (sculpture, bas-relief). I use 3ds max, photoshop. Great experience in working with clients. I develop a design using photoshop, I build models taking into account the filing and shrinkage. Extensive experience in making models by hand, as well as in the manufacture of exclusive jewelry using various technologies (from a piece of metal, by creating a wax model and subsequent casting, filigree, etc.), which is of great importance when building 3D models. Knowledge of the technical process, all stages of production.
